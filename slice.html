Overview

The product was made in an indivisual project at ID. The students were assigned to design a tool box for specific users. I designmed Engardener, a tool box for my father who starts home gardening.

After I made a rapid prototype with cardboards and papers, I created the final model by 3D rendering because the students were not allowed to use the shop due to COVID-19.

Time

Mar.2020 ~ Apr.2020 (5 weeks)

Role

It is my individual project. After researching current houseware products, I've done sketching, rapid prototyping and 3D rendering.
