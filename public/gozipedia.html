Gozipedia

A fun learning app of real creatures through "Kaiju", giant imagenary monsters

Overview

Godzipedia offers children (5 - 12 years old) not only fascinating information about their favorite Kaiju of Godzilla movies but also knowledge about real creatures, such as animals and insects.

Children will gain features of real creatures through characteristics of Kaiju which is attributed to those creatures. They also get knowledge of relationships of those creatures in the real ecosystem by looking through battle results of Godzilla movies.

Time

Sep.2019 (4 weeks)

Role

It is my individual project. After analyzing wireframes of current movie apps, I've done rapid prototyping with Sketch.

Tools

Sketch

Who it's for

Children who are from 5 - 12 years old and love looking at picture books to learn about animals, which remnds me of my childhood when I loved watching Kaiju movies. At that time, many children were excited about the movies because of not only battles among Kaiju but also attractive and diverse Kaiju themselves that were inspired by real animals or insects.

The number of Kaiju movies has decreased currently from when my childhood when a Kaiju movie was shown every year. Hoping that current children will experience enjoyable moments as I did, I integrated an educational function of picture books and exciting Kaiju into a learning app.

UX / UI

The app mainly consists of Kaiju Profile, Animal Profile and Kaiju Battle. After setting up a user profile, the users can choose these three functinalities.

Kaiju Profile and Animal Profile play educational roles to teach animal characteristics. On the other hand, Kaiju Battle plays a entertaining role, while it also educates about natural ecosystem.

Kaiju Profile

On the home screen, children can see Kaiju names and what animals attribute to each Kaiju. The order is changed by Alphabet, Kaiju size and their strength

When children tap a Kaiju picture, the screen moves to a profile page. Its size and evolution process are shown

Features are listed up below size / evolution process. Where the features come from or originate from is shown

When the users tap the original animals on Features, they jump to an animal page to learn about it in details

Animal Profile

Children can start from animal side to learn about animals directly and move to Kaiju pages that animals inspire

Kaiju Battle

Children can play a match up battle game by choosing Kaijus. Based on past battles in historical Godzilla movies, the app shows a battle result. The users can see movie clips of battle scenes, movie reviews and a whole movie.

Not only children enjoy Kaiju battles, they are educated how real creatures live in the natural ecosystem. Who is a predator and a prey in the jungle law? What is in line with scientific facts and what is fake in Kaiju movies? The app tries to make science study more interesting by combining it with fun movie parts.
