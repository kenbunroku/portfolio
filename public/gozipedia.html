Gozipedia

A fun learning app of real creatures through "Kaiju", giant imagenary monsters

Overview

Godzipedia offers children (5 - 12 years old) not only fascinating information about their favorite Kaiju of Godzilla movies but also knowledge about real creatures, such as animals and insects.

Children will gain features of real creatures through characteristics of Kaiju which is attributed to those creatures. They also get knowledge of relationships of those creatures in the real ecosystem by looking through battle results of Godzilla movies.

Time

Sep.2019 (4 weeks)

Role

It is my individual project. After analyzing wireframes of current movie apps, I've done rapid prototyping with Sketch.
