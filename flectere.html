Overview

“Flexible” is derived from Flectere in Latin. Flectere is a personal working desk which can switch between a standing mode and a sitting mode fast and flexibly, by detaching the upper part. This is also so economic that users can put their stuff on the middle board or in the base, and comfortably put elbows on the top when they work.
